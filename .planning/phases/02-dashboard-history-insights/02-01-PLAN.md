## Goal

**As a** user returning to the dashboard, **I want to** see my mood stats at a glance, know if I've logged today, track my streak progress, and see recent entries — **so that** I stay motivated and informed without clicking around.

<objective>
Enhance the dashboard with real data: time-aware greeting, today's status with mood indicator, KPI cards (total entries, avg mood, current streak, entries this week), Quick Log, recent entries list, and streak progress bar. Build server-side streak computation timezone-aware with rest-day allowance.
</objective>
